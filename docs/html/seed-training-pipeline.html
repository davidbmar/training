Overview

This dataset trains a phone receptionist agent for Smith Plumbing. Each conversation is annotated with per-turn phase labels, enabling the model to learn both natural dialogue flow and the structured phases of a service booking call.

Problem Types

8 plumbing scenarios: leak repair, clogged drain, toilet repair, water heater, faucet install, garbage disposal, sewer line, and emergency calls.

Caller Personalities

7 personality archetypes: default, rushed, chatty, angry, confused, elderly, and non-native English speakers. Each shapes tone and pacing.

Conversation Paths

8 resolution paths: happy path booking, no available slots, emergency escalation, DIY recommendation, question only, cancellation, caller hangup, and changes mind.

Phase Annotations

Every turn is labeled with its conversation phase, enabling phase-aware training and evaluation of the agent's ability to navigate structured service calls.

Conversation Phases
